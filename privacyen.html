Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. Facebeneath respects your privacy and is committed to protecting all information you provide to us through the website, email, or other communication channels.

Information We Collect

  • Contact details you provide (name, email, phone number) when sending an inquiry or booking a package.
  • Information about your projects, selected packages, and design preferences.
  • Website usage data if analytical tools (e.g. Google Analytics) are used, exclusively in anonymized form.

How We Use Your Information

  • To respond to your inquiries and communicate regarding projects and services.
  • To create offers and provide professional web and logo design packages tailored to your needs.
  • To improve the quality of our services and website.

Data Security

All information we receive is treated as confidential. We take appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, loss, or misuse.

Sharing of Information

We do not share your information with third parties unless it is necessary to provide the service (e.g. email integration or Google Analytics in anonymized form) or required by law.

Your Rights

You have the right to request access to, correction, or deletion of your personal data. If you wish to exercise your rights or have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: facebeneath@aldindelic.com

Changes to This Privacy Policy

Facebeneath reserves the right to update this Privacy Policy from time to time. All changes will be published on this page.
